About HELPAGE

Founded in Adel, Georgia, HELPAGE bridges the gap between those who need help and those who can provide it. We're not just a lender or charity - we're a community-driven organization that believes everyone deserves a chance at financial stability.

Our unique model combines responsible lending with charitable giving, ensuring that every interaction creates positive impact. When you borrow from us, you're helping fund assistance for others. When you donate, you're building a stronger community.
